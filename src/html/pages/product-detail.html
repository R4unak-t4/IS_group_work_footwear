The Air Jordan 5 Retro brings back the classic silhouette with premium materials and iconic details. Featuring a durable leather upper, mesh panels for breathability, and the signature "shark tooth" design on the midsole.

This iconic basketball shoe offers superior cushioning with visible Air in the heel, a padded collar and tongue for comfort, and a rubber outsole for traction.

Description

The Air Jordan 5 Retro is a legendary basketball shoe that continues to influence sneaker culture today. Originally released in 1990, this iconic silhouette was designed by Tinker Hatfield and worn by Michael Jordan during his 1989-1990 season.

Key features include:

  • Premium leather upper with mesh panels
  • Visible Air-Sole unit in the heel
  • Signature "shark tooth" design on the midsole
  • Padded collar and tongue for comfort
  • Rubber outsole for traction

This retro edition stays true to the original design while incorporating modern comfort technology, making it perfect for both basketball performance and streetwear style.
