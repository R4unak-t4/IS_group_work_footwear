ABOUT LEROY

Our mission is what drives us to do everything possible to expand human potential. We do that by creating groundbreaking sport innovations, by making our products more sustainably, by building a creative and diverse global team and by making a positive impact in communities where we live and work.

Based in Nepal, Kathmandu, LEROY, Inc. includes the LEROY, Converse, and Jordan brands, and is the world's leading designer, marketer and distributor of authentic athletic footwear, apparel, equipment and accessories for a wide variety of sports and fitness activities.

OUR TEAM

We unite diverse perspectives, backgrounds and experiences to create a culture of belonging where everyone can thrive. Our global team is the engine that drives LEROY forward, bringing passion, creativity and determination to everything we do.
