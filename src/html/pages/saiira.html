About Me

I am a visual storyteller based in Pokhara, Nepal. My work focuses on capturing the beauty of Nepali landscapes, culture, and people through photography and visual arts. With a background in fine arts and digital photography, I blend traditional techniques with modern approaches to create compelling visual narratives.

My photographs have been featured in national exhibitions and international publications, including National Geographic Traveler and Asian Photography Magazine.
